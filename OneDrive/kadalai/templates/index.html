How Ocean Guard Works

1

GPS + AIS Tracking

🛰️

24/7 AI-powered monitoring of fishing vessels using GPS and AIS data. Detects suspicious behavior, unusual stops, and dumping patterns.

  • ✓ Real-time position tracking
  • ✓ Abnormal behavior detection
  • ✓ Risk score calculation
  • ✓ Automated alerts
2

Community Spotter Network

👥

Engage fishermen, environmental activists, and coastal communities to report suspicious dumping activities with rewards.

  • ✓ Anonymous reporting system
  • ✓ ₹500 reward per verified report
  • ✓ Mobile app integration
  • ✓ Photo/video evidence support
3

Coast Guard Coordination

🛡️

Real-time coordination with coast guard for targeted inspections of high-risk vessels and law enforcement actions.

  • ✓ Instant alert transmission
  • ✓ Patrol route optimization
  • ✓ Inspection documentation
  • ✓ Legal compliance tracking

Why Ocean Guard?

🌍

Protect Our Oceans

Marine plastic pollution threatens 1 million marine species annually. Ocean Guard provides the tools to prevent dumping at the source.

📊

Data-Driven Insights

AI-powered analytics identify dumping hotspots, track trends, and optimize enforcement resources for maximum impact.

💰

Economic Incentives

Reward clean behavior and community participation. Create sustainable economic models around ocean protection.
